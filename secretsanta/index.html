Secret Santa APP for Android

Take a look at the most simple but powerful Android App for making Secret Santa draws. And it's Free!

Clean & Simple Android App for Secret Santa draws

No more headache about making a draw for Secret Santa. Don't waste your time on writing some papers with the names involved, you always have to repeat the draw because someone takes his own name.

Simple

Create a new group and add participants from your contact list (or manually).

Easy

Make a "New draw". Once realized you can choose to notify participants or not.

Useful

If notified, participants will get the result via email (that includes some ideas for gifts).

Diferent groups for diferent occasions

Install the Secret Santa App in one Android device and then you could create all the groups you need (one for your family, other for your friends, etc).

Then you can add the participants to each group (only need a name and maybe an email). Members can be selected from your contact list or introduced manually.

Just one click to make the draw

Once all participants have been added to the group, you only need to push the New draw button and the draw will be done in a flash.

You can notify participants when the draw has just finished or do it later. Also is possible to notify the participants one by one.

Participants will have their result in their email inbox

If a participant has an email address associated, he could check his result on it. Otherwise, the result can be consulted inside the App. Notified participants will be marked with a check.

Moreover, in the notification email are included useful advices to make the gift purchase easier.
